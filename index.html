Home Brewing Enthusiast

Hi! My name is Dave Bradbury. I live on the Central Coast of NSW, Australia and I'm into brewing and drinking good beer. The aim of this site is to introduce people to the brewing process, share recipes and tips and review professional craft beers. Brewing beer is a great hobby to undertake which only gets more interesting as you develop your skills. From extract brewing to all-grain, it is a creative and rewarding experience. I hope you enjoy the site!

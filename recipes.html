Recipes

Daves XPA

Grain Bill

5.7Kg Pale Malt
0.3Kg Light Crystal

Hop Bill

Citra- 50g
Mosaic- 50g
Galaxy- 50g

Processes

Mash- 66c for 60 min
Boil- 60min
15 litres of water in mash
17 litres of water in sparge
Whirlflock at 15mins
14 days in fermenter
OG 1.051
FG 1.010

Hop Additions

30m- Citra 10g
30m- Mosaic 10g
30m- Galaxy 10g
5m- Citra 20g
5m- Mosaic 30g
5m Galaxy 10g
Dry Hop 7 Days- Citra 20g
Dry Hop 7 Days- Mosaic 10g
Dry Hop 7 Days- Galaxy 30g

This beer came out great. If you like a fruity, mildly bitter ale, give it a whirl.

LC American Pale Ale

Grain Bill

4.2Kg Pale Malt
1.2Kg Munich
0.36Kg Wheat Malt
0.24Kg Crystal 10

Hop Bill

Citra- 20g
Cascade- 65g
Chinook- 75g

Processes

Mash- 66c for 60 min
Boil- 60min
15 litres of water in mash
17 litres of water in sparge at 76c
Whirlflock at 15mins
14 days in fermenter
OG 1.053
FG 1.010

Hop Additions

60m- Citra 20g
20m- Cascade 10g
20m- Chinook 20g
10m- Cascade 15g
10m- Chinook 15g
5m- Cascade 20g
5m- Chinook 20g
0m- Cascade 20g
0m- Chinook 20g

I used the citra at 60mins for bittering. It was left over from another brew so could be substituted for more cascade.
